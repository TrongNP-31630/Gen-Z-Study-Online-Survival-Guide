Tools & Apps to Boost Your Online Learning

You don’t need dozens of apps to study effectively. Pick a few that fit your habits and use them consistently.

Notion

Your digital notebook for everything.

Create a simple study dashboard with your courses, Zoom links, and notes in one place. Keep it minimal so it actually helps you.

Google Calendar

See your whole week at a glance.

Add your online classes, deadlines, and exams. Set reminders so you’re never surprised by a test again.

Forest / Focus To-Do

Stay focused with simple timers.

Use Pomodoro timers to stay focused in 25-minute blocks. Combine with Do Not Disturb mode for maximum focus.

Quizlet / Anki

Remember vocabulary and concepts.

Build flashcards for vocabulary, definitions, and formulas. Review a little every day instead of cramming.

Google Drive / OneDrive

Store and share your files safely.

Organize your notes and assignments in the cloud so you can access them from any device and share with classmates easily.

Pick a few tools and stick with them

Start with two or three tools that match how you already study. Use them consistently for a few weeks and see how they affect your focus and stress levels.
