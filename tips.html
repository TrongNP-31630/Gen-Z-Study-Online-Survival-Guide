Online Study Tips for Gen Z

Here are some realistic tips you can use in your next online study session. Start with one or two, use them for a week, and then add more.

1. Use Pomodoro blocks

Study in 25-minute focus blocks with 5-minute breaks. During each block, concentrate on one task only – no social media or chats.

2. Create a dedicated study place

Choose a fixed spot just for studying. When you sit there, your brain knows it’s time to focus, not relax.

3. Turn off notifications

Use Do Not Disturb or Focus Mode during study sessions so you don’t lose focus every time your phone lights up.

4. Write a to-do list for each session

Before you start, list 3–5 things you want to complete. Clear goals stop you from drifting and wasting time.

5. Take notes and test yourself

Write down key ideas in your own words and quiz yourself afterwards. Active recall helps you remember longer.

6. Take movement and eye breaks

Every 45–60 minutes, stand up, stretch, drink water, and look away from your screen for a few minutes.
